Last reviewed: 18 July 2026

Earn relevant coverage, links, relationships and qualified ecommerce conversations by making Spires Digital's existing tools and practical experience genuinely useful to an outlet's audience. This is an outreach register, not permission to send messages or name clients.

## Outreach rules

1. Lead with one useful asset, observation or dataset—not the agency biography.
2. Tailor the angle to a named audience and explain what the reader can do with it.
3. Link to the most useful tool or evidence page, not automatically the homepage.
4. Keep the 15+ years, £10m+ spend and 150+ campaign claims, but label them as cumulative experience and never imply they predict a result.
5. Do not identify a client, share source data or offer a reference without written permission.
6. Do not mass-send the same pitch. Record the owner, date, response and next action.
7. Add a tracked campaign link only after the recipient and destination are agreed.

## 30-day delivery sequence

### Days 1–5: prepare

- Capture clean screenshots and a 30-second explanation for the profit calculator, waste estimator and VAT guide.
- Draft three short pitches: local business utility, ecommerce trade insight and affiliate measurement.
- Prepare a one-page founder fact sheet linking to the public author profile, credential context, editorial policy and selected outcomes.
- Ask relevant clients whether they are willing to be a private reference; do not publish names by default.

### Days 6–12: local relevance

- Contact Digital Greenhouse with the clinic or workshop proposal.
- Send the Guernsey Press business desk a concise, local and data-led story option.
- Submit a useful Contact Magazine outline rather than a promotional company profile.

### Days 13–20: platform partnerships

- Confirm current Awin and Shopify partner contacts and their accepted educational formats.
- Offer the affiliate margin framework to the most relevant partner channel.
- Package the ecommerce calculator as a merchant education resource with limitations stated clearly.

### Days 21–30: trade editorial and learning

- Pitch one original, audience-specific story to Retail Gazette or another named ecommerce editor.
- Prepare the Search Console study for publication only when the 28-day comparison is available.
- Review replies, referral sessions, tool completions, consultation starts and qualified opportunities.
- Keep, improve or stop each angle based on evidence; do not judge the programme by backlinks alone.

## Measurement

Record for every outreach item: outlet, named contact, asset, angle, send date, reply, placement, referral sessions, tool completions, consultation starts, qualified consultations and follow-up decision. Use the site's existing consent-aware GA4 events and CRM/source fields; do not send calculator inputs or private client data to analytics.

- Cross-cluster interlinking between related topics

## Interlinking Rules
1. Every article links to its parent service page
2. Every article links to at least 2 other articles (noted with # numbers above)
3. Every article ends with a CTA linking to /contact or Calendly
4. Articles reference case studies where relevant (/case-studies)
5. Cross-cluster links connect related topics (e.g., Shopify + Google Shopping)

## Content Specifications
- 1,200-2,000 words each
- H2/H3 structure for featured snippets
- FAQ section with schema on commercial-intent articles
- tip-box callouts for actionable advice
- British English throughout
- Current 2026 platform features and data
